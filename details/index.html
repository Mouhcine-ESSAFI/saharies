Overview

If you're visiting Marrakech and have limited time, don't miss the opportunity to experience a magical night under the stars during our Marrakech Desert Trip. Our 2-day excursion to the Zagora Desert offers an unforgettable journey through the High Atlas Mountains and the famous Tizi-n-Tichka pass, bringing you to the captivating desert landscape of Zagora.

During this desert tour from Marrakech, you'll be treated to breathtaking views as you travel. Capture stunning photographs of your group against the backdrop of Berber villages, picturesque valleys, and majestic snow-capped mountains. Explore Morocco's renowned kasbahs: Telouet and Ait Ben Haddou, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Additionally, take the chance to visit the famous Ouarzazate film studios, known for their iconic movie sets.

Immerse yourself in the allure of the Marrakech Desert Trip, where adventure and enchantment await amidst the vast desert.

FAQs

When and where is the pick-up scheduled for my Marrakech to Zagora desert tour?

The pick-up is scheduled at around 8 AM from your Riad or hotel in the city. If you are arriving on the same day, the trip can start directly from the airport. Our transportation team will contact you the night before to confirm the exact details.

What is the maximum number of people for the trip?

This is a private tour, so the number of participants can vary. The cost will adjust according to the number of people, as outlined in our pricing table. We will ensure that the vehicle provided is appropriately sized for your group. Contact us for additional information.

Can we book the tour before finalizing our hotel reservation in Marrakech?

Absolutely, you can book your Morocco desert tour and put TBC (To Be Confirmed) in the hotel pick-up section. Email us your hotel information in Marrakech as soon as you finalize your booking to update the pick-up details. You can also use Whatsapp!

Does Marrakech to Zagora private desert tour come with local guides?

Our Marrakech to Zagora 2 days desert tour do not include local guides. For instance, a local guide at Ait Benhaddou Kasbah is optional and not part of the regular package.

Are special dietary accommodations available on the Marrakech to Zagora 2 Days Desert Tour?

Yes, we can adjust the menu for vegetarians, vegans, and other dietary preferences on the Marrakech to Zagora 2 Days Desert Tour. Please notify us at the time of booking.

Do we get meals during the Marrakech to Zagora desert trip?

Yes, the trip package includes breakfast and dinner as part of the half-board offering.
